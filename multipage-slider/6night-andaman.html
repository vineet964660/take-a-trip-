Andaman Packages

6 Night Andaman

SAMPLE ITINERARY: (PORT BLAIR 4NT – DAY TRIP TO BARATANG + HAVELOCK 1NT + NEIL ISLAND 1NT)

PORT BLAIR – CORBYN’S COVE BEACH - CELLULAR JAIL - LIGHT & SOUND

Day 01:  Assistance on arrival at Veer Savarkar Airport, Port Blair and transfer to the hotel. Check in to your hotel on arrival. Later start the amazing trip to Corbyn’s Cove Beach, one of the most picturesque, coconut palm fringed beach, 7 kms away from Port Blair town, and to the Cellular Jail, formerly known as Kala Pani, it was a colonial prison used by the British to deportee political prisoners to this remote location. In the evening is scheduled for enjoying the famous Light and Sound Show at Cellular Jail. Return to the hotel. Overnight in Port Blair.

PORT BLAIR – ROSS ISLAND – NORTH BAY ISLAND

Day 02:  Post breakfast excursion trip to Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Island (Ross Island), It was the Capital of Port Blair during British and Japanese regime, prior to India’s Independence. Later we will visit North Bay, It is also called “The Gate Way to Port Blair”   Overnight in Port Blair.

PORT BLAIR – HAVELOCK ISLAND – RADHANAGAR BEACH

Day 03: As per your ferry schedule board a ferry from Port Blair and get transferred to Swaraj Dweep (Havelock Island) (62 kms by sea from Port Blair). On reaching, get transferred to the hotels to complete the check-in. Enjoy your afternoon at the World Famous Radha Nagar Beach (Beach number 7) 'Best Beach in Asia'. Overnight in Havelock.

HAVELOCK – KALAPATHAR BEACH - NEIL ISLAND – LAXMANPUR BEACH

Day 04:  Post breakfast check out from the hotel and visit to Kalapathar Beach. Later take the ferry as per schedule from Havelock and get transfer to the Neil Island. Assistance on arrival on Neil Island Neil Island is a marvelous Island with plenty of marine life, an ideal location for eco-tourism. Neil Island is triangularly shaped with lust green surroundings of paddy fields, banana plantations and tropical trees. Check in to your hotel. Later visit to Laxmanpur Beach for the magnificent sunset viewing. Overnight in Neil Island.

NEIL ISLAND – HOWRAH BRIDGE – BHARATPUR BEACH - PORT BLAIR

Day 05:  Post Breakfast check out from the hotel and visit to Howra Bridge, a natural bridge formation. Later visit Bharatpur Beach, ideal for Snorkeling and glass bottom boat rides (for Extra cost) you can see the beautiful coral and underwater life. Later take the afternoon ferry from Neil Island and get transferred to Port Blair. Overnight in Port Blair.

PORT BLAIR – BARATANG (SIGHTSEEING) – PORT BLAIR (BY ROAD – 4 HRS EACH WAY)

Day 06: Early morning excretion trip to Baratang. A boat ride through thick mangrove forest with mangrove canopies blocking sunlight. Ride to the Lime stone caves in Baratang Island is exciting as well as thrilling. 110 km by road from Port Blair – between south and middle Andaman, Baratang islands brings forth the tropical side of the Andamans. Mangroves, birds, caves, trekking, and local tribes. Overnight in Port Blair.

PORT BLAIR - DEPARTURE

Day 07: Post breakfast take the departure transfer to Port Blair airport for your onward journey.

Transport uses in Andaman: AC or Non AC Xylo / Ertiga exclusively for the clients – (As per itinerary).

Ferry Uses in Andaman: Govt. Ferry / Makruzz / Green Ocean / Aashi Ferry
