Kolkata Packages

3 NIGHT KOLKATA

SAMPLE ITINERARY: (KOLKATA 3NT)

KOLKATA – ARRIVAL - SIGHTSEEING

Day 01: Assistance on arrival at Railway Station / Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Airport, Kolkata and Transfer to the hotel. Check in to the hotel on arrival. Later visit Kolkata local sightseeing Kalighat Temple, Victoria Memorial, Indian Museum, Babughat Riverside. Overnight in Kolkata.

KOLKATA – GANGASAGAR

Day 02: Early morning starts a full day excretion trip to Gangasagar. Gangasagar or Sagar Island is an island in the Ganges delta, lying on the continental shelf of Bay of Bengal about 100 south of Kolkata. The island of Gangasagar is among the most popular Hindu pilgrimage places in the country. Evening return back to hotel. Overnight in Kolkata. Overnight in Kolkata.

KOLKATA – SIGHTSEEING

Day 03: Post breakfast visit Kolkata local sightseeing Dakshineswar temple, Eco park, Wax Museum, Biswa bangla Gate. Return to the hotel. Overnight in Kolkata.

KOLKATA – DEPARTURE

Day 04: Post breakfast take the departure transfer to Kolkata Airport / Howrah Railway Station for your onward journey.
