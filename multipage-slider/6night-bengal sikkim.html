North-East Packages

6 NIGHT NORTH BENGAL & SIKKIM

SAMPLE ITINERARY: (GANGTOK 3 NT + LACHUNG 1NT + LACHEN 1NT + DARJEELING 1 NT)

NJP RLY STATION / IXB AIRPORT AIRPORT - GANGTOK (APPROX - 130 KMS 4 HRS)

Day 01:  Assistance on arrival at New Jalpaiguri Railway Station / Bagdogra International Airport and transferred to Gangtok from here 4 & ½ hrs journeys through the valley of hills and river reach by evening check in at hotel evening free for leisure suggested to visit M.G. Marg The Mall Road & to nearby shopping Complex overnight at Gangtok.

EXCURSION TO TSOMGO LAKE AND BABA MANDIR (53 KMS. / 3 HRS. ONE-WAY)

Day 02: Post breakfast, start for an excursion to Tsomgo Lake (12,400 ft. / 37 Kms. / 02 Hrs. from Gangtok) and to Baba Mandir (13,200 ft. / 16 Kms. / 01 Hrs. from Tsomgo Lake). Afternoon back to Gangtok. Overnight stay at Gangtok.

GANGTOK - LACHUNG (APPROX - 117 KMS 6 HRS)

Day 03:  Post breakfast around 9.00 a.m. take the transfer to Lachung (8610 ft). On your way visit Singhik View point, Seven Sister Waterfalls, Naga Water Falls. Lunch on the way. Check-in to your hotel on arrival. Overnight in Lachung.

LACHUNG - YUMTHANG (11800 FT.) - LACHEN (Approx – 65 kms 2 ½ hrs)

Day 04:  Post breakfast embark on an excursion trip to Yumthang-valley (known as Valley of Flowers) and back. On your way back visit Hot Spring. Afternoon could be spent interacting with the local people / visit to the local market later take the transfer to Lachen. Rest of the day free or at own to explore Lachen. Overnight in Lachen.

LACHEN – SIGHTSEEING - GANGTOK

Day 05:  Post breakfast we embark on an excursion trip to Thangu, Gurudongmar Lake (17200 ft) & Chopta Valley. Later take the transfer to Gangtok. Overnight in Gangtok

GANGTOK – DARJEELING - HALF DAY SIGHTSEEING

Day 06: Post breakfast take the transfer to Darjeeling (6,950 ft.) “The Queen of the Hills”. Check-in to your hotel on arrival. Later visit Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, P.N. Zoological Park (Thursday close), Tenzing Rock, Ropeway, Tibetan Refugee self-help Centre (Sunday closed), Tea Garden (outer view). Evening free or can be spent at the Mall (Chowrastha). Overnight in Darjeeling.

DARJEELING – SIGHTSEEING - RLY STATION / IXB AIRPORT (75 KMS. / 3 HRS.)

Day 07: Early morning start for Tiger Hill to watch the spectacular sunrise and it’s amazing effects on the Mt. Early morning (4:00 AM) visit Tiger Hill, Ghoom Monastery and Batasia Loop. Later as per scheduled departure take the transfer to Ixb Airport (Bagdogra) for your onward journey.
